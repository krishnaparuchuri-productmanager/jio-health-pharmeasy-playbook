# Health Platform x ePharmacy: 100M User Growth Strategy 💊

## Strategic Fit

**The Opportunity:** Telehealth platforms and licensed ePharmacy networks are natural complements — yet most operate in silos.

- **Health Hub (EHR/Consult App):** Active daily users for consultations, diagnostics, and health records
- **ePharmacy Partner:** Medicine delivery, discount vouchers, and last-mile fulfillment network

**The Gap:** No seamless *consult → prescription → pharmacy delivery* loop exists today.

## 12-Month Growth Playbook

| Quarter | Focus | Tactics | KPIs | ePharmacy Role |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Q1 | Acquisition | 1-tap Rx transfer post-consult | +10M users | API integration + launch discounts |
| Q2 | Retention | AI symptom → consult → auto-cart | 35% weekly actives | Predictive cart fill |
| Q3 | Revenue | Medicine bundles in health wallet | ₹300Cr ARR | Co-billing integration |
| Q4 | Network | Clinic/dark store pickup network | 100M DAU | 1,200+ city coverage |

## Integration Roadmap

1. **Post-Consult Rx Flow** — Doctor issues e-prescription → auto-populates patient's pharmacy cart with coupon applied at checkout
2. **AI-Assisted Pharmacy** — Symptom checker → urgency triage → real-time stock check → delivery ETA surfaced within the health app
3. **Super App Health Tab** — Unified interface for consultations, lab reports, medicine reorders, and chronic care refill reminders
